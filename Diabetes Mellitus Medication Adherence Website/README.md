# MedAdhere: Diabetes Mellitus Medication Adherence Website

**MedAdhere** is a digital health solution aimed at improving medication adherence for people with diabetes mellitus. The website provides valuable information about diabetes, medication adherence, and strategies for staying on track with prescribed treatments. It includes educational resources, practical tips, and interactive tools to help users manage their health better.

## Features

- **Educational Content**: Learn about diabetes types (Type 1, Type 2, and Gestational Diabetes), causes, symptoms, and related complications.
- **Adherence Strategies**: Practical tips and tools for improving medication adherence, including pill organizers, medication reminder apps, and social support.
- **Interactive FAQs**: A dynamic FAQ section answering common questions about diabetes management and medication.
- **Video Resources**: Educational videos about diabetes care and the importance of medication adherence.
- **Downloadable Resources**: Access printable medication trackers and other resources to help manage diabetes effectively.
- **Testimonial Section**: Real-life stories from people living with diabetes and their medication adherence journeys.

## How to Use

1. Visit the website [MedAdhere](https://your-website-link.com) to explore information about diabetes, adherence strategies, and more.
2. Browse the **Tools & Resources** section to download helpful materials, such as medication trackers.
3. Use the **Medication Adherence** strategies to create a routine that fits your lifestyle and ensure you never miss a dose.
4. Check out the **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)** for additional tips and advice on managing diabetes.

## Tech Stack

- **HTML5**: For the structure and content of the website.
- **CSS3**: For styling the layout, including responsiveness and animations.
- **JavaScript**: For interactive features, including the FAQ section and modal testimonials.
- **External Libraries**:
  - **Botpress Webchat**: For providing real-time chat support through a chatbot.
  - **YouTube Embed**: For integrating educational videos directly into the page.
